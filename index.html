About Our Company

Founded in 2008, Raj Enterprises has grown from a small workshop to a leading metal fabrication company known for quality craftsmanship and innovative solutions. Our team of certified welders, engineers, and fabricators bring over 15 years of combined experience to every project.

We specialize in custom metal fabrication for commercial, industrial, and residential applications. From architectural metalwork to heavy structural fabrication, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional results.

Our 15,000 sq. ft. facility is equipped with state-of-the-art CNC machinery, robotic welding systems, and precision cutting tools to handle projects of any scale with efficiency and precision.
